Non-Academic Activites

Quidditch Teams

Join one of the four Quidditch teams at Hogwarts
and practice flying, scoring goals, and participating
in friendly matches.

Dueling Club

Hone your magical dueling skills in the Dueling
Club. Learn defensive and offensive spells, and
engage in duels with fellow students.

Magical Creatures Care

Join the Magical Creatures Care Club to learn about
and care for various magical creatures. Participate
in feeding sessions and caretaking duties.

Wizard Chess Tournaments

Compete in monthly Wizard Chess Tournaments,
showcasing your strategic and tactical skills in the
wizarding world's version of chess.

Enchanted Gardens

Explore the Enchanted Gardens and learn about
magical plants. Attend workshops on herbology,
potion ingredients, and magical gardening.

Wizarding Photography Club

Join the Wizarding Photography Club to capture
magical moments at Hogwarts. Learn about
magical photography techniques!
