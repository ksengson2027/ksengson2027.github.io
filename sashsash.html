A Glimpse of Forevermore

(sonnet)



As the sun rises, within your embrace,
Even when the sky turns gray, desire in me stays bold.
Ensnared by the glimpse of your face glowing gold,
Adoration fills each breath I take.


With each hazel gaze of golden keepsake,
“I have found peace and world in you, my solace”, you composed.
Thoughts of you shine like the sun's warm blaze, I uphold.
Enamored by your beauty, truly the cure of my ache.


Enchanted by your seraphic glow in my shadowed life,
Delightfully guiding me to the heart of your crown.
You're the best part, a dream that came into view.


A timeless film of our story profound,
A sweet harmonic melody, amidst life’s strife,
In a world forever, me and you.
