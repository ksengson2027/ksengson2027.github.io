The Silent Whispers of Once Ember Glow

(sonnet)



In season’s change, love’s flame began to fade,
A vibrant rhythm slowed, turned icy cold,
Each verse a whisper, a story to be told,
Where echoes lingered, where passion once played.


In the depths of time, our love’s essence swayed,
Like autumn leaves, it turned to shimmering gold,
In cycles of mistakes, the past couldn’t hold,
Now tainted with wounds that won’t evade.


We tended the fire, yet darkness veiled the light,
Bound in ties, we strove to persist,
No need to escape, the truth we couldn’t fight.


In circles, memories linger, from the moment we kissed,
Releasing the love we couldn’t ignite,
Once vibrant, now dimmed, in shades of gray, we exist.
