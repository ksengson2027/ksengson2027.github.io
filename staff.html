Godric Gryffindor

Godric Gryffindor was one of the founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for his bravery and chivalry, Godric created Gryffindor House, where students are valued for their courage and determination. Gryffindor's symbol is the lion, and its colors are red and gold.

Helga Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff was known for her dedication to equality and acceptance of all magical talents. She founded Hufflepuff House, where students are valued for their hard work, loyalty, and fair play. The house symbol is the badger, and its colors are yellow and black.

Rowena Ravenclaw

Rowena Ravenclaw valued intelligence and wit, and her house attracted those with a thirst for knowledge. Ravenclaw House, founded by Rowena, values wisdom and learning. The house symbol is the eagle, and its colors are blue and silver.

Salazar Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin prized cunning and ambition, and his house sought out those with pure-blood wizarding heritage. Slytherin House values resourcefulness and cunning. Its symbol is the serpent, and its colors are green and silver.

~ administrators ~

Meet the dedicated administrators who contribute to the success of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Dumbledore is the Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. A highly skilled wizard, Dumbledore is known for his wisdom, kindness, and commitment to protecting the magical world from dark forces. As the leader of the school, he plays a vital role in shaping the education and values of young witches and wizards.

Minerva McGonagall

Minerva McGonagall is the Deputy Headmistress of Hogwarts and the Head of Gryffindor House. With her strict exterior and love for her students, she is also the Transfiguration professor. Professor McGonagall is known for her intelligence, discipline, and unwavering dedication to the well-being of the students.

Severus Snape

Severus Snape is the Potions Master at Hogwarts and the Head of Slytherin House. His expertise in the Dark Arts and his mysterious demeanor make him a complex figure at Hogwarts. Professor Snape, although strict, plays a crucial role in the protection of the school and its students.

Filius Flitwick

Filius Flitwick is the Charms professor at Hogwarts and the Head of Ravenclaw House. As a skilled wizard, Professor Flitwick brings charm and humor to his classes. He is passionate about teaching young witches and wizards the art of spellcasting and magical charms.

Pomona Sprout

Pomona Sprout is the Herbology professor at Hogwarts and the Head of Hufflepuff House. Professor Sprout is known for her expertise in magical plants and her nurturing personality. She encourages students to appreciate the magical world of plants and nature.

~ teachers ~

Meet the dedicated teachers who impart knowledge and wisdom to the students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Aurora Sinistra

Aurora Sinistra is the Astronomy professor at Hogwarts. With her passion for celestial studies, Professor Sinistra introduces students to the wonders of the night sky and the magical properties of celestial bodies.

Rolanda Hooch

Madam Rolanda Hooch is the Flying instructor and Quidditch referee at Hogwarts. She teaches students how to ride broomsticks and ensures fair play during Quidditch matches.

Remus John Lupin

Remus Lupin is the Defense Against the Dark Arts professor at Hogwarts. Despite facing challenges due to his condition as a werewolf, Professor Lupin is a knowledgeable and compassionate teacher.

Rubeus Hagrid

Rubeus Hagrid is the Keeper of Keys and Grounds at Hogwarts. He has a deep love for magical creatures and serves as the Care of Magical Creatures professor. Hagrid is known for his kind heart and loyalty.

Sybill Trelawney

Professor Sybill Trelawney is the Divination professor at Hogwarts. She possesses the gift of second sight and teaches students about predicting the future through various magical methods.
